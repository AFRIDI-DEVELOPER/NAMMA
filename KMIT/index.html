About KMIT

Shaping the Future of Healthcare

Kabir Medical Institute of Technology (KMIT) is a premier healthcare education institution in Tauru, Nuh District, Haryana. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap in quality medical education, KMIT has grown into a beacon of academic excellence and clinical expertise.

Our state-of-the-art campus provides students with an immersive environment that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training in modern laboratories and clinical settings.

Admission Process

A simple, transparent process to help you begin your journey at KMIT.

01
📝

Submit Application

Fill the application form online or visit campus with your academic details.

02
📄

Document Verification

Submit marksheets, ID proof, photos, and category certificates for verification.

03
🏆

Merit Selection

Shortlisting based on academic merit. Receive your offer letter via email/SMS.

04
💳

Fee Payment

Pay admission fee online or via DD. Scholarships available for eligible students.

05
🎓

Enrollment

Collect your ID card, welcome kit, and begin your healthcare education journey!

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ 10+2 with Science (PCB/PCM) for degree programs
  • ✅ Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • ✅ 10+2 with any stream for DMLT & GNM
  • ✅ Age: 17–25 years at time of admission

Documents Required

  • 📄 10th & 12th Mark Sheets & Certificate
  • 📄 Migration Certificate
  • 📄 Character Certificate from last institution
  • 📄 6 Passport-size Photographs
  • 📄 Aadhar Card / Voter ID
  • 📄 Category Certificate (if applicable)
