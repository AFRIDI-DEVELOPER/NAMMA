About KMIT

Shaping the Future of Healthcare

Kabir Medical Institute of Technology (KMIT) is a premier healthcare education institution located in the heart of Tauru, Nuh District, Haryana. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap in quality medical education in rural India, KMIT has grown into a beacon of academic excellence and clinical expertise.

Our state-of-the-art campus, affiliated with leading universities, provides students with an immersive learning environment that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training in modern laboratories and clinical settings.

Our Programs

Courses We Offer

Comprehensive medical and paramedical programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in healthcare.

🔬

B.Sc. Medical Lab Technology (BMLT)

A 3-year undergraduate program training students in advanced clinical laboratory diagnostics, pathology, and biomedical analysis.

3 YearsDegree
🧪

Diploma in Medical Lab Technology (DMLT)

A 2-year diploma program focusing on essential lab techniques, sample analysis, and diagnostic procedures for healthcare settings.

2 YearsDiploma
💊

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma)

A 4-year professional degree in pharmaceutical sciences covering drug formulation, pharmacology, and clinical pharmacy practices.

4 YearsDegree
🩺

GNM Nursing

A 3-year General Nursing & Midwifery program preparing compassionate, skilled nurses for hospitals and community care.

3 YearsDiploma
🫀

B.Sc. Cardiac Care Technology

Specialized 3-year program in cardiac diagnostics, ECG interpretation, echocardiography, and cardiovascular patient management.

3 YearsDegree
📡

B.Sc. Radiology & Imaging

A 3-year program covering X-ray, CT scan, MRI, and ultrasound imaging technologies for advanced medical diagnostics.
